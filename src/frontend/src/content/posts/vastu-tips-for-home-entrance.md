In Vastu Shastra, the main entrance of your home is called the **Mukha Dwara** — literally, the face of the house. It is the primary channel through which cosmic energy (**prana**) enters your living space. Getting the entrance right is one of the highest-impact Vastu corrections you can make.

## The Best Directions for a Main Entrance

Not all directions are equal in Vastu. Here is a ranking based on traditional Vastu texts and modern practice:

### North-Facing Entrance (Best for Wealth)
The north direction is governed by **Kubera**, the lord of wealth. A north-facing entrance is considered highly auspicious for financial growth, career advancement, and abundance. Families in business especially benefit from this orientation.

### East-Facing Entrance (Best for Health & Growth)
The east is where the sun rises — it brings vital solar energy into the home. An east-facing entrance promotes health, vitality, and new beginnings. It is ideal for young families and professionals.

### North-East Entrance (Highly Auspicious)
The north-east (**Ishan**) corner is considered the most sacred zone in Vastu. An entrance here invites spiritual growth, wisdom, and divine blessings.

### Avoid South-Facing Entrances
A south-facing main door is associated with challenges, delays, and obstacles. If you have a south-facing entrance, specific Vastu remedies can neutralize the negative effects without demolition.

## 7 Powerful Vastu Tips for Your Main Entrance

### 1. Keep the Entrance Well-Lit
A well-lit entrance activates positive energy flow. Install bright lights — ideally warm white or golden — above the main door. A dark entrance repels prosperity.

### 2. Use a Solid, Auspicious Main Door
Your main door should be the largest and most beautiful door in the house. Solid wood doors (teak or sheesham) are ideal. The door should open inward (into the house), not outward.

### 3. Place a Threshold (Doorstep)
A raised threshold (doorstep) is an important Vastu element — it symbolically prevents energy from draining out. Keep it clean and intact.

### 4. Avoid Clutter Near the Entrance
Shoes, broken items, dustbins, and clutter block the flow of positive energy. Keep the entrance area spotless and organised.

### 5. Add Auspicious Symbols
Hang a **Swastika** or **Om** symbol above the main door. A **Toran** (marigold garland) during festivals activates welcoming energy. These are time-tested Vastu practices.

### 6. Correct Door Squeaks Immediately
A squeaky or creaking door symbolises friction and obstacles in Vastu. Oil the hinges immediately. The door should open smoothly and silently.

### 7. Keep the Entrance Free of Mirrors Facing the Door
A mirror directly facing the main entrance reflects incoming energy back out, reducing prosperity. If you have one, cover it or reposition it.

## Vastu Remedies for South-Facing Entrances

If your main entrance faces south, don't panic. These time-tested remedies help:

- **Lord Ganesha idol** above the main door (facing inward)
- **Swastika** in red painted on both sides of the door frame
- **Red Pyramid Yantra** buried at the doorstep threshold
- **Wind chimes** with 9 rods hung near the entrance
- Consultation with a qualified Vastu expert for personalized remedies
